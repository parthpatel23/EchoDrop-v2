Privacy Policy

EchoDrop respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Information we collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account data: name, email address, and password (hashed).
  • Scheduled messages: recipients, content, selected platform, and schedule time.
  • Integration data: tokens or IDs from third‑party services (e.g., Gmail, Twilio, Telegram) required to send messages.

2. How we use your information

  • To create and manage your EchoDrop account.
  • To schedule and deliver messages on your behalf.
  • To improve and monitor the reliability of the service (logs, error tracking).

3. Data sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share your data only with third‑party providers that are needed to deliver your messages, such as:

  • Email providers (e.g., Gmail API).
  • SMS and WhatsApp providers (e.g., Twilio).
  • Telegram Bot API for personal reminders.

4. Data retention

Scheduled messages, logs, and account data are stored as long as your account exists or as required for academic demonstration purposes. You can request deletion of your account and associated data.

5. Security

We use environment variables for secrets, hashed passwords, and restricted API access. However, no system is 100% secure, especially in an academic project context.

6. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us via the Contact page.
